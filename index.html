capabilities

Everything a
data scientist does.

Without the hiring process, the onboarding, or the $180K salary.

AI-Written Data Pipelines
SBfinance inspects your schema and generates production-quality Python code to clean, transform, and prepare data. Handles nulls, outliers, type mismatches — automatically.
AutoML Model Building
Trains multiple candidate models, runs cross-validation, selects the best performer, and explains why. Regression, classification, time-series forecasting — all covered.
Risk & Anomaly Engine
Monitors model confidence, detects feature drift, surfaces statistical anomalies, and flags patterns that deserve human attention before they escalate.
Auto-Generated Dashboards
Produces publication-ready charts: distributions, trends, correlation matrices, confusion matrices, feature importance — adapted to your specific data and model type.
Executive Reports
Generates structured PDF reports combining model metrics, risk signals, charts, and plain-language explanations — ready for stakeholders who don't speak Python.
RAG Chat on Your Data
Ask anything in plain language. "Which customers are highest risk?" "What drove the Q3 spike?" SBfinance answers using your actual data as context — not generic AI guesses.
