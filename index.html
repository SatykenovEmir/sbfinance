The problem

Most businesses already have the data. They just cannot use it fast enough.

Finance teams and founders often work across CSV exports, Excel files, accounting records, and disconnected reports. The data exists — but turning it into clear answers still takes too much manual work.

Too much reporting work is still manual

Teams spend hours collecting files, cleaning numbers, rebuilding views, and checking inconsistencies before they can even start analysis.

Decisions happen before the reporting catches up

By the time the report is ready, the team may already be late to react to a cost spike, cash pressure, or changing revenue pattern.

Data is hard to access for non-finance people

Founders and operators need answers now, but too often they must wait for static reports or manually search across spreadsheets.

The solution

SBfinance turns raw finance files into decision-ready clarity.

Instead of building heavyweight workflows from scratch, SBfinance gives teams a faster way to understand performance, ask better questions, explore scenarios, and spot risks earlier.

Bring in data → ask questions → understand the numbers → decide what to do next.

SBfinance is designed as a finance copilot experience that combines data ingestion, natural-language interaction, forecasting logic, and dashboard-style outputs in one simple product flow.
